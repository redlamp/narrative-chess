### Why these specific files vs. waiting

`bun create next-app` writes into `app/`, `package.json`, `tsconfig.json`, `next.config.*`, `.gitignore`, `README.md`. It does NOT write to `lib/`, `middleware.ts` at root, `.env.local.example`, or `supabase/`. Pre-staging in those untouched paths means scaffold runs cleanly afterward and merging this branch into `feat/scaffold-next` is conflict-free.

`app/(auth)/`, `app/diagnostics/`, `app/page.tsx` overrides are the parts I deliberately did NOT pre-stage — they live in scaffold's territory.
