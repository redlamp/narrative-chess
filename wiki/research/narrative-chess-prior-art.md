# Narrative Chess Prior Art

**Type:** games + apps survey
**Captured:** 2026-05-09
**Source:** assembled from web searches during the project-name branding audit (see related decision note)

Prior art and competitor scan run while evaluating whether "Narrative Chess" is a defensible project name. Two reasons it's worth keeping: (1) shows what the genre already does, (2) flags name collisions to avoid when picking a new name.

Summaries below paraphrase store-page descriptions; treat them as research, not endorsements.

## Direct name conflicts (literal "Narrative Chess" or near-clones)

### Narrative-Chess (GitHub, bh679)
**Source:** https://github.com/bh679/Narrative-Chess
**Status:** archived 2026-03-08, read-only, 0 stars / 0 forks / 65 commits
**License:** CC-BY-SA-4.0
A narrative-driven chess game built to practice working with Claude. Browser-based, full chess rules via chess.js, Stockfish WASM AI (100–3200 ELO), Chess960, multiple piece art styles, replay viewer, local-first localStorage with optional server sync. Defunct but indexed on Google for "narrative chess" queries.

### Chess Story (iOS app)
**Source:** https://apps.apple.com/us/app/chess-story/id6740005728
A chess game that walks players through key moments in chess history. Each level concludes with a duel against a legendary chess figure. Story-mode framing aimed at beginners.

## Story-driven chess (genre-adjacent)

### Story Time Chess
**Source:** https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/story-time-chess-thinking-cup-games/1139900166
**Publisher:** Thinking Cup Games (2021)
A board-game-plus-instruction-system that teaches chess to young children using silly stories, character-illustrated pieces, and a custom board. Each piece type has its own personality and story explaining its movement rules. Established the "narrative chess for kids" niche.

### Chessaria: The Tactical Adventure
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/575340/Chessaria_The_Tactical_Adventure_Chess/
A mix of chess, tactical setup, and story-based missions. Adventure Mode runs ~100 solo levels (20+ hours). Probably the highest-effort narrative-chess hybrid currently shipped.

### Dark Chess
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/1151130/Dark_Chess/
Story-driven chess campaign. Custom boards, traps, and rule-breaking pieces. Fully narrated adventure framed as duels against legendary masters.

### Stonemachia
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/2982610/Stonemachia/
**Release:** 2026-05-26 (Steam)
Players control Zefiro, a pawn able to transform into other chess pieces, on a Dantesque journey through a world ravaged by the "Plague of Angels." Strong narrative framing, painterly art.

### Idle Chess Story
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/1067110/Idle_Chess_Story/
Idle-game built on chess units with special abilities. Players assemble a team, climb the ranks, win the Grand Tournament. Narrative is light progression flavor more than story content.

### Chess Corp
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/2071270/Chess_Corp/
Pixel-art puzzle game that frames AI-generated chess puzzles inside a "solve the mystery of your existence" wrapper. Narrative is metafictional rather than character-driven.

### Chess Chronicles (Chrome extension)
**Source:** https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/chess-chronicles/edabpcobcjanlgidfaoafobcgaahehlj
Browser extension that surfaces historically famous chess games and the stories behind the players. History-as-narrative angle rather than fictional story.

### Checkmate Chronicles
**Source:** https://apps.apple.com/us/app/checkmate-chronicles/id6476261188
iOS app described as a "unique journey through chess one day at a time" — daily-puzzle framing wrapped in a journey/chronicle metaphor.

### Fun Chess Saga
**Source:** https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ducalstudio.funchesssaga
Google Play. Magical-themed chess with special powers, "magical world where strategy and fun collide." Casual mobile slot.

### Chess Defense Saga
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/2287360/Chess_Defense_Saga/
Steam. Tower-defense crossbreed using chess pieces and anime characters with synergies. Genre-adjacent, name-adjacent.

## Adjacent products that own candidate rename slots

These don't compete with the chess game directly, but they own the SLD or store listing for names we considered. Listed so a future rename audit can skip them quickly.

### Marginalia (horror game)
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/889280/Marginalia/
**Developer:** Connor Sherlock (2014, remade 2017)
First-person horror set in Kestlebrook valley. Two interweaving plotlines about loss and time. $5.99 on Steam, also on itch.io. Different genre from chess but indexed for the name.

### Vellum (literary roguelike)
**Source:** https://store.steampowered.com/app/917950/Vellum/
Co-op roguelike about defending books, with class-defining colors and a spellbook. Literary aesthetic. Owns vellumgame.com.

### Vellum: The Game Master's Workbench
**Source:** https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/vellum-dm/vellum-your-gm-co-pilot
AI-powered TTRPG world/campaign manager. Different domain from chess but eats the "Vellum" namespace further.

### Caissa (chess-mythology namespace)
The chess goddess of poetry — directly fits the "narrative chess" concept, which is exactly why it's saturated:

- **Caissa Chess Software** — https://caissachess.net/ (tournament management)
- **Caissa.com** — long-running chess server
- **Caissa: Novel Chess** (Android app) — https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.xima.caissaxima
- **Caïssa Chess Island** (Steam GUI) — https://store.steampowered.com/app/1648660
- **Caissa Chess Academy** — https://caissachess.org/
- **CAISSA AI Chess Coach** by CircleChess — https://circlechess.com/
- **Caissa Chess Store** — https://www.caissa-chess.com/

The mythology is too good and too over-claimed; treat Caissa as effectively unavailable.

## What this surfaces

The narrative-chess concept is well-occupied at every tier:

| Tier | Examples | Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Children's tabletop | Story Time Chess | Different audience; not a competitor |
| Premium narrative campaign | Chessaria, Dark Chess, Stonemachia | Direct genre — well-funded studios |
| Mobile story-mode | Chess Story | Closest competitor in concept + casual player tier |
| Light/idle/casual | Fun Chess Saga, Idle Chess Story, Chess Corp | Crowded SERP for "chess + [story word]" |
| Historical chronicle | Chess Chronicles, Checkmate Chronicles | Owns the "chronicle" framing |
| Tournament + AI tools | Caissa\* family | Owns the chess-mythology namespace |

Two practical takeaways for the v2 project:

1. The literal name "Narrative Chess" is descriptive (so weakly defensible) and overlaps both an archived but indexed GitHub repo with the same concept and an active App Store listing with the closest framing (Chess Story). Rename early — cost grows with each milestone.
2. Naming candidates that combine "[adjective] Chess" or "Chess [noun]" mostly land in already-occupied SERPs. Single-word ownable names (e.g., Pawnsong, Folio, Stanza) and metaphor-first names from the manuscript / poetry world have the cleanest discovery space, with the trade that "this is chess" needs to ride along in the tagline.
